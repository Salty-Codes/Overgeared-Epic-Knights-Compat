EpicOverKnights Changelog
=========================

1.0.0 (2026)
------------
- Initial release
- Compatibility recipes for all Epic Knights weapon types using Overgeared materials:
  forging, casting, and assembly
- Supported materials: Iron, Gold, Bronze, Copper, Tin, Steel, Silver, Stone
- Supported weapon types: Claymore, Zweihander, Lochaberaxe, Guisarme, Ahlspiess,
  Lucernhammer, Heavymace, Heavy Crossbow, and more
- Armor set compatibility: Kastenbrust, Burgonet, Cuirass, and additional sets
- Removes default vanilla crafting recipes for Epic Knights items where replaced
  by Overgeared recipes
